05 — Knowledge Base

Everything you need to know about how our referral engine operates, pricing, and what is required from you.

Is Referral Hub completely free to use?

Yes. Core referrals, access to the live job board, and membership in the Telegram community are 100% free. We believe everyone deserves a foot in the door. Our Premium tier is entirely optional and is designed for those who want personalized, 1-on-1 career coaching, resume rewrites, and mock interviews.

Exactly how does the referral process work?

It's a simple 3-step process. First, browse the live job board and find a role that matches your skills. Second, submit a request via our platform. Third, our system routes your profile to a verified employee working at that specific company. They review your profile and submit you through their internal corporate portal. You will receive an official email directly from the company's ATS.

Are the referrers actually genuine employees?

Absolutely. Trust is our core metric. Every single referrer on our platform goes through a strict manual verification process. We cross-reference their corporate email addresses and LinkedIn profiles to ensure they are actively employed at the company they claim to represent.

How long does it take to get a referral submitted?

Standard requests are usually processed within 3-5 business days, depending on the volume and the specific employee's availability. Premium members receive priority routing, which guarantees submission or feedback within 48 hours.

I am a fresher. Can I still get referred?

Yes! We have a dedicated section on our job board specifically for freshers, university graduates (Batch 2026/2027), and early-career professionals. Many of our partner employees actively look to refer strong junior talent for entry-level roles and internships.
